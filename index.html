A Sweet Dream
Worth Sharing

Nestled in Lake Wylie, Dreamery Creamery was born from a simple love of handcrafted ice cream done right. We take pride in every scoop — real flavors, generous portions, and a space that feels like home.

From our signature sundaes piled high with whipped cream, nuts, and a cherry, to build-your-own creations and hand-spun milkshakes — everything here is made to be remembered.
